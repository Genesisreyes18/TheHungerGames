THE HUNGER GAMES

ABOUT

The story follows a first-year student getting ready for class registration. She has her dream course list open, backup classes written down, laptop charged, Wi-Fi checked, a second device nearby just in case, and even a timer counting down the final seconds. When the time comes, she takes a deep breath and clicks the important “Complete Course Registration” button. Then there are three options for what might happen.

In one case, she gets all four of her chosen classes, jumps up with joy, dances around her room, and sends excited messages to her friends. In another version, she gets only two classes. She quickly checks her backup list, calls her friends for advice, and looks through social media and websites for reviews of other professors. In the last case, she gets none of the classes she wanted. She feels sad and frustrated but does not give up. She reaches out to the Academic Resource Center, her friends, second plan, and reads reviews for the class from the student groups, hoping she can figure out a new schedule.
